Impossibly Easy Cheeseburger Pie

It's just like a cheeseburger but eaten with a fork!

Ingredients

  • Cooking spray
  • 1 pound lean (at least 80%) ground beef
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup Original Bisquick mix
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degress C). Spray 9-inch glass pie pan with cooking spray.
  2. Cook beef and onion in a nonstick skillet over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until beef is browned and crumbly, 8 to 10 minutes.
  3. Meanwhile, whisk milk, Bisquick, and eggs together in a small bowl.
  4. Drain beef and onion mixture; transfer to prepared pie pan. Stir in salt, sprinkle with Cheddar, and pour Bisquick mixture over the top.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until a knife inserted in the center comes out clean, about 25 minutes.
